Peanut Butter Avocado Toast

Description

Nothing says creamy like a peanut butter and avocado combo!

Thought it couldn't get better? Just pair that with the crunch of toasted bread, and it in fact gets better.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
    • Creamy
    • Chunky
  • Avocado
    • Sliced
    • Mashed
  • Toast
    • White
    • Wheat
    • Sourdough

Steps

  1. Toast slice of bread of choice.
  2. Spread peanut butter over toast.
  3. Slice or mash avocado and add to toast.
