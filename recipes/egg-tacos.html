Egg Tacos

Description

What's breakfast without eggs? What's breakfast without bacon!? What's life without cheese!!1?

This minimalistic recipe will guide you to the meat and potatoes of a savory breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla
    • Wheat
    • Corn
      • Yellow
      • White
  • Your favorite cooking oil

Steps

  1. Bake bacon strips in the oven for 20 minutes.
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a pan until shimmering.
  4. Add beaten eggs into the pan.
  5. Divde eggs among the tortillas.
  6. Add bacon and cheese.
