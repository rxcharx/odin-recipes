Overnight Oats

Description

Follow this simple guide to a quick yet satiating breakfast that takes less than 5 minutes to prepare the night before. Overnight oats takes the oatmeal that you know and love and transforms it into a creamy, refreshing cold treat to start off your morning without the hassle!

The recipe below is a classic, but that doesn't mean that it's strict; practically every ingredient can be swapped to your base and to your toppings to make it your meal.
